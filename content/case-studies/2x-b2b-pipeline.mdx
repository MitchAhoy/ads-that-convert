<CaseStudySection title="Context">
The client had consistent traffic but inconsistent pipeline quality. Sales feedback showed lead intent mismatches and delayed follow-up loops.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Strategy">
We restructured campaigns around stage-aware intent groupings and aligned conversion tracking to opportunities rather than soft leads.

Creative and landing copy were revised to pre-qualify users before submission.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Outcome">
The account reached 2x qualified pipeline in under two months while keeping lead efficiency stable enough to continue scaling spend.
</CaseStudySection>
