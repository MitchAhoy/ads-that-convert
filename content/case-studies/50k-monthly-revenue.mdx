<CaseStudySection title="Context">
The company entered paid acquisition with no mature campaign history and needed to build traction quickly without sacrificing lead quality.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Strategy">
We launched with tightly scoped campaign sets, fast weekly testing cycles, and strict budget reallocation based on observed revenue contribution.

As conversion confidence improved, we expanded into adjacent intent segments while preserving attribution clarity.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Outcome">
The account progressed from launch to $50,000+ in monthly revenue while maintaining visibility into which keywords and campaigns were driving pipeline and closed revenue.
</CaseStudySection>
