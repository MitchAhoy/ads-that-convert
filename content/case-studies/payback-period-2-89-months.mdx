<CaseStudySection title="Context">
This is a B2B AI SaaS product in a rapidly growing vertical with strong demand and high search volume.

The client wanted to see meaningful results quickly and ensure long-term scalability.

Because the product doesn&apos;t offer a free trial, it was critical that we targeted high-intent, ready-to-buy users to generate fast results.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Strategy">
We built the account from scratch, launching brand-new campaigns with targeting focused on generic terms, competitors, features, brand, and specific use cases.

Our build strategy focused on:

- Starting with high-intent **exact match** keywords to avoid wasting ad spend on irrelevant queries.
- Using **manual bidding** to gather initial conversion data without inflated CPCs.
- Targeting **desktop-only** traffic to increase conversions, ensuring users could sign up and demo the product immediately, without the drop-off often seen on mobile.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Results">
We started seeing sign-ups as soon as the campaigns went live, with purchases coming in within the first week.

In just 30 days, we generated **558 sign-ups**, with **42 converting into paying customers**, resulting in **$2,219 in new MRR**. 70% of which came from **generic, non-branded keywords**. These are entirely **net new customers**.

The payback period continues to fall and currently sits at **under 3 months for non-branded terms**. We&apos;re now scaling with increased budgets, refining keywords, and expanding targeting around top-performing terms.
</CaseStudySection>
