<CaseStudySection title="Context">
This SaaS company needed growth that could be sustained on realistic unit economics from the first month of spend.

The target was not just lead volume. It was quality-adjusted pipeline and fast payback.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Strategy">
We built campaigns around narrow, high-intent keyword clusters and tightened conversion definitions so bidding optimized toward revenue-driving actions.

We paired this with ongoing search-term governance to reduce low-intent traffic and protect cost per qualified signup.
</CaseStudySection>

<CaseStudySection title="Outcome">
Within 30 days, campaigns generated $2,219 in new MRR while holding a 2.89-month payback period, creating a solid baseline for controlled scaling.
</CaseStudySection>
